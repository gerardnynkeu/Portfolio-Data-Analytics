## Dataset Overview


The dataset contains weekly sales data from multiple Walmart stores across different time periods. It includes the following attributes:

Store: Unique identifier for each store.

Date: The week of sales recorded.

Weekly_Sales: The total revenue generated by the store in a given week.

Holiday_Flag: A binary indicator (1 = holiday week, 0 = non-holiday week).

Temperature: The average temperature during the recorded week.

Fuel_Price: The cost of fuel per gallon in the storeâ€™s region.

CPI (Consumer Price Index): An economic indicator representing inflation.

Unemployment: The regional unemployment rate.

## Limitations 

While this analysis provided valuable insights, several limitations should be considered:

- **Data Completeness & Quality:** 
  - No missing values were present, but potential data inconsistencies could introduce biases.

- **Feature Limitations:** 
  - The dataset lacks customer demographics, marketing data, and external economic factors that impact sales.

- **Holiday Influence Simplification:** 
  - Holiday effects are flagged but do not account for regional variations, promotions, or seasonality beyond binary classification.

- **External Market Conditions:** 
  - Economic trends, competitor pricing, and supply chain disruptions are not included, limiting market context.

- **Time Scope Constraints:** 
  - The dataset covers a limited period, restricting insights into long-term trends and shifting consumer behavior.

- **Granularity of Data:** 
  - Aggregated sales data may obscure store-specific or product-level trends critical for targeted analysis.

---

Recognizing these limitations ensures **analytical transparency** and highlights areas for future refinement in **predictive modeling** and **business insights**.
